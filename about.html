About Us

Where premium recreation meets energy, comfort, and community.

Cassi Sports & Recreational Center Pvt. Ltd. is built to deliver a polished sports and leisure experience in Bharatpur, Chitwan — combining active play, indoor safety, modern ambience, and strong brand identity.

Company Introduction

A destination that feels active, premium, and refreshingly modern.

Cassi Sports is more than a place to play. It is a thoughtfully built environment where sports, leisure, and social time come together in a way that feels elevated and enjoyable.

From futsal and cricsal to swimming and indoor recreation, our goal is to give visitors a memorable, high-quality experience with comfort, convenience, and visual appeal.
